## 🔐 Privacy & Confidentiality Guidelines

### Before Sharing Any Document:

✅ **DO:**
- Remove sensitive personal information (SSN, phone, personal address)
- Get permission from the issuing organization
- Redact confidential company information
- Verify format is PDF for security
- Check expiration dates on certificates

❌ **DO NOT:**
- Share documents with active passwords
- Expose colleague/manager personal information
- Include confidential business strategies
- Share unredacted salary/compensation details
- Post documents without permission
